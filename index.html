About Me

Hi! I'm Abdul-Barcky, a passionate front-end developer with a love for clean design, smooth user experiences, and building things that solve real problems. I specialize in crafting responsive, accessible websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

When I'm not coding, I'm often exploring design trends, diving into open-source projects, or writing about tech. My goal is to keep learning and collaborating with people who want to make the web better. Whether it's building from scratch or improving existing platforms, I enjoy turning ideas into interactive, usable interfaces.

Featured Projects

Here are some of my recent projects that showcase my skills in front-end development and design. Each project reflects my commitment to clean code, responsive design, and user-centered experiences.

Aquaminder is a responsive product landing page built to promote a smart water bottle designed to track your hydration levels in real-time. This project focuses on clean layout, strong call-to-actions, and modern UI/UX principles, ensuring users are informed and encouraged to convert.

Neon Future is a digital magazine layout inspired by futuristic tech trends and storytelling. Designed using a grid-based layout, this page highlights how visual hierarchy and strong typography can be used to capture readers' attention in an online format.

This blog page tells the quirky story of a squirrel with a caffeine addiction through creative writing and engaging design. With custom illustrations and a storybook feel, the page uses humor and storytelling to create an immersive reading experience.

This travel blog template showcases a modern and photo-focused design for travel enthusiasts. Featuring responsive image galleries, elegant typography, and user-friendly navigation, the site is built to inspire wanderlust and provide tips to fellow travelers.

This digital piano, built as part of a freeCodeCamp project, blends clean design with interactive functionality to offer a fun, browser-based musical experience. Styled to resemble a real piano, it invites users of all skill levels to explore music through a simple and responsive interface. Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, it showcases how code can bring creative, real-world concepts to life in a sleek and educational way.
